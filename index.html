Game of War + Unit Test

Game Rules:

  • Deal 26 cards to 2 players from a Deck of 52 cards
  • Iterate through turns where each Player plays a card
  • The player who played the higher card is awarded a point and the losers card
  • Ties result in zero points for each player and both players discard cards
  • The game ends when one player runs out of cards
Winner has the most points.
